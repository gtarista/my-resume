About Me

I'm a Service Desk Analyst at Datacom in Auckland — my first formal enterprise IT support role, and a deliberate step in the direction I've been building toward. Before this, I spent 2+ years as an IT Technician at Echo Technology Lifecycle Solutions, processing and troubleshooting high volumes of endpoint hardware in an ITAD environment. That's where I got real with Windows, BIOS/boot handling, hardware diagnostics, and device prep at scale.

My background in Computer Engineering (University of San Carlos, Philippines) gave me a systems-thinking foundation that goes deeper than most entry-level support candidates. I hold Microsoft 365 Fundamentals (MS-900) and I'm working toward desktop support, endpoint support, and junior systems pathways.

Outside of work I run a two-node homelab on Proxmox — self-hosted services, Docker containers, VLAN networking, and NAS storage — because I learn best by actually building things and breaking them. I also built an internal tool at Echo that automated device data extraction from Blancco reports, cutting hours of manual data entry. I don't just follow process; I look for ways to make it better.
